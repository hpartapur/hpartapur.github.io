Buildings on Campus

Masjid Zahra Mahad Zahra Ewaan Burhani Saahat Mawaid Badriyah Maktabat Auditorium Academics Admin Maskan & Rabwat

Masjid Zahra

Masjid Zahra is the main mosque on campus, where students and faculty gather for daily prayers and Friday congregational prayers.

Mahad Zahra

Mahad Zahra is the hub for Quranic development.

Ewaan Burhani

Ewaan Burhani is a multi-purpose building that houses various administrative offices, including Maktab Tanzeem and Marhala Saqafat Aama.

Saahat

Saahat is a spacious outdoor area where various events, including Aljamea's annual convocation ceremony, take place.

Mawaid Badriyah

Mawaid Badriyah is the main dining hall on campus, where students and faculty gather for daily meals.

Maktabat

Maktabat is the main library on campus, housing a vast collection of books and resources for students and faculty.

Auditorium

The Auditorium is a large, multipurpose space used for a variety of events, including lectures, conferences, and performances. It has a seating capacity of 1000 people and is equipped with state-of-the-art audio and video systems.

Academics

The academic building houses the various departments of the Al Jamea tus Saifiyah. It includes classrooms, computer labs, and faculty offices. The academic building is where students attend lectures and complete assignments.

Administration

The administration building houses the offices of the various administrative departments of the Al Jamea tus Saifiyah. This includes the registrar's office, financial aid office, and other administrative offices. This is where students go for administrative assistance.

Maskan & Rabwat

The maskan & rabwat building houses the student living quarters for male and female students respectively. It includes dining halls and recreation areas for the students. It provides a comfortable living experience for the students during their time at Al Jamea tus Saifiyah.
