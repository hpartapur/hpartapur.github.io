Maktab al-Talim

Responsible for creating an academic environment in Aljamea, and undertaking all activities that lead to the creation of this environment. All academic departments function under Maktab al-Talim. It implements guidelines regarding academics and implements policies as received from Omara Aljamea. It monitors the timely covering of course content and quality of teaching.
