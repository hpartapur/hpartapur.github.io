About ☰

Khardal, inspired by the New York Times' Wordle!
The game developed with the intention of strengthening one's Lisan u Dawat vocabulary.
Khardal in Arabic means mustard seed (known in Lisan u Dawat as رائي نو دانه.)
The game has one simple principle: Guess the word!

Use the keyboard provided to create a Lisanuddawat word. Once you're ready, press "Enter".
The boxes for each letter will then light up gray, yellow, or green.
If the letter is gray, then this letter is not present in the correct answer.
If the letter is yellow, this letter is present in the answer, but not in the place you've put it.
If the letter is green, you've placed the letter in the correct position!
Keep guessing until all the boxes are green! Be careful though, you only have 6 guesses.
